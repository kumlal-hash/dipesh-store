ℹ️ About Dipesh Store

Dipesh Store is a trusted local agricultural food shop located in the heart of Kalimati, Kathmandu — one of Nepal's busiest fresh produce markets. We bring you the finest locally grown vegetables and agricultural products sourced fresh every morning.

Our mission is simple: provide the Kathmandu Valley with fresh, healthy, and affordable agricultural produce while supporting local farmers. We sell potatoes, onions, chillies, garlic, tomatoes, and many more seasonal vegetables.

Whether you visit us in person at Kalimati or order online, we are committed to quality and fast delivery. Call us at 9849149553 or place your order above!
